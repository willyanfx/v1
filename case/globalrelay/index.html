Redefining SaaS solution

One of the responsibilities at GlobalRelay has been designing for Archive10 - a 6 years old high-performance cloud archiving platform designed to meet the privacy, compliance, and security needs of the financial sector and other highly regulated industries. Used primarily by compliance teams from Scotiabank, General Societe, HSBC, stockbrokers, etc.

Design Process

Soon the product owners and managers communicate the business requirements, we start with fast moving ideas. Iterating quickly until we get it right. This part of our work of whiteboarding ideas, create user-flows, vet the best of these and nail down an overall direction — it’s about trying new things quickly and moving on from what isn’t working. Whittle down the top ideas into lo-fi wireframes, and validate those ideas based on technical feasibility, impact, and innovation.

The following step is moving our strongest ideas into simple animations and interactions. Then we'd test those, refine our designs, and ship something great. Some of the features follow these steps are dashboard report page and the revamp of the Archive 10 main navigation that is a working in progress.

Design System

Start

Our initial challenge in building Design System has been to reunite a product that had scaled faster than expected, by creating a base to address the growing needs of GlobalRelay web apps. Our library is both a learning tool and compass to correct our course as we build new things.

React Components

In addition to design user interfaces and interactions, I've been collaborating in the component code base. The process of creating components that grant the applications both visual and functional consistency, helping to make the users feel at home and easily navigating their way to complete the desired interactions with the product.
