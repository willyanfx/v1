Archive 10Web app

Redefining Saas solution

One of my major responsibilities whilst at GlobalRelay has been designing for Archive10 - a 6 years old high-performance cloud archiving platform designed to meet the federation, compliance, privacy, and security needs of the financial sector and other highly regulated industries. Used primarily by compliance teams from Scotia Bank, General Societe, HSBC, stockbrokers, etc.

Paper first

As soon as the product owners and managers communicate the business requirements, we start white-boarding the user-flows and rough sketches of my initial thoughts and design ideas. User-flows, journey maps, and storyboards are key to make sure that designs fulfill all of the requirements and user needs before I take the idea over to the wire-framing process and prototyping.

Making

Lately, I have been collaborating in creating report dashboard graphs and enhancing navigation throughout of the web app with the goal of making it simpler. Every UX and visual design decision was made with this in mind — and was viewed through the lens of helping organizations get to a point of productivity sooner. It was my intention that Archive 10 should naturally adapt to the way you work best, and remain easy-to-use regardless of content. By far the biggest challenge was to ensure that Archive 10 appeared visually simple, even when dense with information and content.

UI Prototyping

New functionality was introduced where it made sense, and usually in response to a shift in user expectations. Prototyping was important for example to determine the ideal placement of text and how animation could indicate success without being too over-the-top.
